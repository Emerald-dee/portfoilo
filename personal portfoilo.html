Excerpts & About

Sigurd lost everything the day the cursed shell was found. His wife Alicia lies in an endless sleep, his daughter Linnea was claimed by a word the world itself could not hold, and his spirit teeters on the edge of breaking.

But in the Silver Plains, where eternal mist coils and shadows whisper, the truth of that day waits to be uncovered. The shell is not merely a relic-it is a key, a prison, a question the world itself cannot answer.

From Seraphine, the fallen angel with a crimson halo, to Darcey, bound by vows older than kingdoms, to Roland, Kaelen, and the kings who shape nations-every figure holds a fragment of truth. Yet every revelation only deepens the most dangerous mystery:

Who is the real villain-the fallen, the faithful, or the broken?
